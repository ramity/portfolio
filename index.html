About Me

Hello! I'm Lewis Brown, a machine learning engineer with a passion for building innovative solutions using cutting-edge technologies. With 3+ years of commercial and academic experience in the field, I have a strong background in designing, developing, and deploying machine learning models for a range of applications.

Throughout my career in software, I've worked on a variety of projects that involved developing models for image recognition, object detection and localization, and limited computation, edge device environments. I'm skilled in a range of programming languages, including Python, C++, and Java, and have experience working with a variety of frameworks such as TensorFlow, PyTorch, scikit-learn, and Keras.

With my academic research background, I'm passionate about staying up-to-date with the latest developments in the field and have a keen interest in exploring new research and techniques. One of my most exciting projects to date was applying my thesis work to an edge computing evironment without the use of a ML library, which required a high degree of persistence, investigation, and creativity to execute.

In my free time, I enjoy a variety of hobbies and interests. I'm an avid reader and spend a lot of time online reading articles and books about a variety of topics. I'm also passionate about strength training, which helps me stay physically and mentally fit. When I'm not working out or reading, I enjoy programming and building personal projects. I find it both challenging and rewarding to create something from scratch and bring my ideas to life. To facilitate this, I have a home lab where I can deploy and utilize various software environments. I also enjoy building and customizing computers and commonly do so for many of my friends. Lastly, I'm an avid gamer and love to challenge myself with difficult games. These hobbies help me unwind and keep my mind engaged outside of work.

If you're looking for a talented and driven machine learning engineer to help take your projects to the next level, I'd love to hear from you. Please feel free to get in touch to learn more about my experience and skills.

The project had many unique challenges. We were only API-like details of the internal MQTT system at BMW.
